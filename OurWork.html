There are 41,415 species that are endangered and 16,306 that are on the verge of extinction. A few animals that are endangered would be, leopards, elephants, ferrets, pandas and penguins. On the“red list” consisting of animals close to going extinct would be rhinos, gorillas, tigers, and polar bears.

Any contribution to this cause can help protect and save the thousands of animals that are endangered or close to extinction. Your time and support can make a difference.
